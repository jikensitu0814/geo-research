# AIO / SERP Literature Notes

## Xu et al. 2026

Source: Xu et al., *Measuring Google AI Overviews: Activation, Source Quality, Claim Fidelity, and Publisher Impact*.

- arXiv HTML: https://arxiv.org/html/2605.14021v1
- arXiv abstract: https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.14021

The closest external comparison is Section 4.2, `Reference overlap with first-page results`. The paper reports that, averaged across 7,583 AIOs, AIO reference domains overlap with first-page top 5 results at 25.0%, top 10 at 41.4%, and the full first page at 70.2%. It also reports that 29.8% of AIO reference domains do not appear anywhere on the corresponding first page.

This public module observes AIO-to-SERP domain overlap of 25.38% for SERP top 3 and 62.26% for SERP top 10. The samples are not equivalent: Xu et al. use Google Trends trending queries, while this module uses a commercial tracking sample.
